Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an age limit to join Shambhala?
We welcome students starting from 6 years of age and beyond — there is no upper age limit! Anyone with the spirit to learn is encouraged to join.
Do I need prior experience in Karate?
Not at all! We train beginners as well as experienced martial artists. Our instructors tailor guidance according to each student’s level.
