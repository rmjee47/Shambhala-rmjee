FAQs

Who can join Shambhala? Is there an age limit?

Shambhala welcomes everyone aged 6 and above. Whether you're a child, adult, or senior, you can begin your martial journey with us.

Do I need any prior experience in martial arts?

No prior experience is needed. We offer programs for all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

What style of Karate is taught at Shambhala?

We teach traditional Shotokan Karate with emphasis on both sport and philosophical aspects of martial training.
